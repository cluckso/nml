# NeverMissLead — Pricing Overview

Simple, usage-based pricing built for service businesses.
No setup fees. No contracts. Cancel anytime.
Overage on all plans: $0.10/min after included minutes.

---

## Free Trial (All Plans)

- **100 free call minutes**
- No time limit
- No setup fee
- No overage during trial
- Service pauses when minutes are used
- Upgrade to any plan to continue

Designed so businesses can test real calls—not demos.

---

## Basic — $99/month

*Best for solo operators & small shops*

**Annual option:** $990/year (2 months free, paid upfront)

**Includes:**
- Up to 500 call minutes / month
- 24/7 AI call answering (business hours + after-hours logic)
- Caller name, phone number, and reason captured
- Call summaries via SMS and/or email
- Missed-call recovery
- Custom greeting with business name

**Setup:** 100% self-serve onboarding · No setup fee

---

## Pro — $199/month

*Best for growing service businesses*

**Includes everything in Basic, plus:**
- Up to 1,200 call minutes / month
- **Industry-optimized AI agents** (user selects business type from a dropdown; system links the correct prebuilt intake flow)
- Service-specific intake flows (plumbing, HVAC, electrical, etc.)
- Appointment request capture (date/time preferences)
- Emergency vs non-emergency detection and routing
- SMS confirmation to callers
- Full call transcripts + summaries
- Email and CRM forwarding

**Setup:** Automated onboarding using industry templates · No setup fee

---

## Local Plus — $299/month

*Built for high-volume, multi-department trades*

**Includes everything in Pro, plus:**
- Up to 2,500 call minutes / month
- Priority call routing
- Multi-department logic (sales vs service, by trade, or by team)
- After-hours emergency handling with escalation logic
- Lead tagging (emergency, estimate, follow-up)
- Weekly usage & lead reports
- Fully branded AI voice (tone, pacing, greeting)

**Setup:** Automated department creation and AI voice tuning · No setup fee

---

## Why This Pricing Works

- Minute-based trial ensures fairness and real value delivery
- No setup fees reduce friction and increase conversion
- Annual Basic option improves cash flow and reduces churn
- Industry-optimized Pro agents feel "custom" without custom labor
- Clear upgrade ladder based on call volume and complexity
- Consistent overage rate keeps pricing transparent
