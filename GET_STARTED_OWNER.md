# What Happens After Sign-Up (Service Business Owner)

## 1. Sign up

- Create an account (email/password). After sign-in you can start a **free trial** or go to **Pricing** to choose a plan.

---

## 2. Start free trial (recommended) or choose a plan

- **Free trial:** No card required. Add your business phone on `/trial/start`, then complete onboarding.
- **Paid plan:** On **Pricing**, pick Solo / Team / Pro and complete Stripe checkout, then onboarding.

---

## 3. Onboarding (plan-aware)

- Set up your business: industry, name, address, business hours.
- **Phone setup (one place):** Enter your **business line** (for forwarding to your call assistant). Call summaries are sent by email (and SMS when configured).
- Fields like departments, CRM, and after-hours emergency only show when they're included in your plan (Team / Pro).

---

## 4. Dashboard — connect your call assistant

- On the dashboard you'll see **"Connect your call assistant"**.
- Click **"Connect to my call assistant"**. The app creates your voice agent (Retell) and, when available, assigns a phone number.
- After connecting, the same card shows **phone setup in one place:**
  - **1. Business line — forward to this number** (with step-by-step instructions).
  - **2. Your number for call alerts** (the one you set in onboarding).

Forward your business line to the CallGrabbr number (via your carrier or Retell). Then your call assistant answers forwarded calls and you get alerts at the number you set.

---

## 5. After that

- **Incoming calls** to your forwarded line are answered by your call assistant (business name, hours, intake).
- **You get** call summaries by email and/or SMS (based on settings). **Calls** in the app show transcripts, lead tags, etc.
- **Billing** tracks minutes and overage ($0.22/min after included minutes). **Weekly reports** (Pro) by email.

**Flow:** Sign up → free trial or plan → onboarding (plan-based fields, one phone setup) → dashboard → **Connect to my call assistant** → forward your line to the number shown.
