# CallGrabbr Android — Release Notes

## Version 122.0.0 (versionCode 122)

### What's new
- **Mobile dashboard** — View call stats, recent leads, and trial status from your phone
- **Call history** — Search and filter captured calls, including urgent/emergency flags
- **Appointments** — See scheduled appointments from AI-booked calls (Team & Pro plans)
- **Push notifications** — Get alerted when new leads come in (enable in Settings)
- **Pull to refresh** — Swipe down to update dashboard, calls, and appointments
- **Dark theme** — Polished UI designed for quick checks between jobs

### Improvements
- Faster load times and smoother navigation
- Haptic feedback on key actions
- Deep links from notifications into the right screen

### Requirements
- CallGrabbr account (sign in with your web dashboard credentials)
- Android 5.1+ (API 22)

## Google Play listing copy

**Short description**
Monitor leads on the go. View calls, appointments, and stats from your CallGrabbr dashboard. Push alerts for new leads.

**Full description**
CallGrabbr Dashboard keeps your AI answering service in your pocket.

• Real-time stats — total calls, minutes used, urgent flags
• Call log — every captured lead with caller details
• Appointments — upcoming bookings from your AI receptionist
• Push notifications — never miss a hot lead
• Settings — notification preferences on device

Sign in with your existing CallGrabbr account. Works with Solo, Team, and Pro plans.
